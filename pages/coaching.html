Coaching

Kyle is a respected acting coach whose students have done everything from book leading roles in their school shows, to receive acceptance into their dream college/conservatory, to booking professional roles across the United States. As the Youngest 2-Time Helen Hayes Award Winning Actor in history, Kyle brings a unique perspective of youth and experience to his coaching. Kyle offers a FREE 15 minute intro consultation session, and competitive pricing of $30/30 minute session and $50/60 minute session.
