So, What's Your Process?

Podcast Host

Kyle is one half of “So, What’s Your Process?” The show where Kyle and his mother, Best-Selling Author Ally Dalsimer, sit down with creatives from all different fields to talk about what they do and how they do it. With guests ranging from Oscar-Shortlisted Filmmakers to Broadway and TV Stars to Celebrity Photographers and more, the show gives viewers and listeners a chance to dive into the mind of some of the most creative people in the world.
