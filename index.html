Meet Kyle

Kyle is the Youngest 2-Time Helen Hayes Award Winning Actor in history. His credits range from socially anxious teenager Evan Hansen, to Wall Street serial killer Patrick Bateman, to SpongeBob SquarePants, and everything in between. Kyle has performed Off Broadway and across the United States, leading productions with character-first performances described as “Remarkable”, “Heart-Wrenching”, and “Award-Winning and Riveting Times a Thousand”. He resides in New York City and is a proud member of Actors Equity Association.
